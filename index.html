doSmartMove

How it Works:
  • A smart contract is created between the shipper & the trucking service on the ETH Blockchain
  • Once delivery has been completed. The Smart Contract is executed and funds are released to the trucking service
Benefits:
  • Drivers can now maximize the unused space on their trucks.
  • Smart Contracts are public and open to the terms. No middle can hide or change the terms.
  • Truckers can now be paid in full once completed. No longer will they have to wait for Net Term to be paid.
